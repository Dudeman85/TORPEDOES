Decodes a 3- or 4-channel UHJ signal into a B-Format signal with FuMa channel ordering and UHJ scaling. For 3-channel, the 3rd channel may be attenuated by 'n', where 0 <= n <= 1. So to decode 2-channel UHJ, supply 3 channels with the 3rd channel silent (n=0). The B-Format signal reconstructed from 2-channel UHJ should not be run through a normal B-Format decoder, as it needs different shelf filters.

Implements DecoderBase.
