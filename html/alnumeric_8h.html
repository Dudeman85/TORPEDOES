◆ fastf2i()

int fastf2i ( float f)
inlinenoexcept

Fast float-to-int conversion. No particular rounding mode is assumed; the IEEE-754 default is round-to-nearest with ties-to-even, though an app could change it on its own threads. On some systems, a truncating conversion may always be the fastest method.
