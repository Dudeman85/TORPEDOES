int32_t Assimp::MDL::Skin::group

0 = single (Skin), 1 = group (GroupSkin) For MDL3-5: Defines the type of the skin and there fore the size of the data to skip: 2 for 565 RGB, 3 for 4444 ARGB, 10 for 565 mipmapped, 11 for 4444 mipmapped (bpp = 2), 12 for 888 RGB mipmapped (bpp = 3), 13 for 8888 ARGB mipmapped (bpp = 4)
